        Industry Standard Paper Recommendations

        Flyers & Booklets

        60lb Cover 70lb Matte Cover 80lb Cover Glossy options available

        Perfect for promotional materials, event flyers, and multi-page booklets that need durability without excessive weight.

        Professional Photos

        Kromekote Photo Paper 80lb Gloss Cover 100lb Gloss Cover

        High-quality photo reproduction requires glossy finishes and heavier weights for professional presentation and longevity.

        Brochures & Catalogs

        70lb Matte Cover 80lb Cover 100lb Cover Glossy options available

        Marketing materials need substantial feel while remaining easy to fold and distribute. Matte finishes reduce glare for easy reading.

        Business Cards & Postcards

        14pt (110lb Cover) 16pt (120lb Cover) Glossy options available

        Professional networking materials require premium weight and finish to make lasting impressions. 16pt is the gold standard.

        Menus & Greeting Cards

        80lb Cover 100lb Cover 110lb Cover

        Items handled frequently need durability and premium feel. Heavier weights resist wear and convey quality.

        Standard Documents

        20lb Text 28lb Text

        Daily office printing and color documents. 28lb provides better opacity and professional feel compared to standard 20lb paper.
